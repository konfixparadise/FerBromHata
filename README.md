# 🚀 About
Welcome to the official FerBromHata web application — the digital home of our brand and the place where customers, partners, and curious visitors can explore everything we do. Our company built this platform to share who we are, what we offer, and what kind of world we are working towards: a future where farms are sustainable, automated, and friendly to both people and animals.

The website is split in four main views:
- **Home**: a quick introduction to our company, our values, and live statistics about the platform.
- **Catalog**: a full library of our solutions, organised by tags, with filtering and a built-in search to help you find exactly what fits your farm.
- **About**: the full story of FerBromHata — our history, our location, and detailed app statistics for the curious.
- **Admin Panel**: our internal "Studio", where our content team can update catalogs, add new products, and manage users — without ever touching the code.

The whole experience is designed to feel modern, calm, and easy to use, with our signature dark green colour palette and the Bromus grass logo always close at hand.

# ✨ Versions

## V1.0 — Assignment 2 release (May 2026)
This is our first major release after the original showcase, and we are very proud of it! V1.0 transforms FerBromHata from a static showcase site into a fully interactive web application. Here is what we added:

- **User accounts.** Visitors can now sign up, log in, and log out. Each new user gets their own profile, including a customisable profile picture (or a default avatar if they prefer not to upload one).
- **Admin panel ("FerBromHata Studio").** Inspired by YouTube Studio, this is a separate, protected area where our admins manage the company's content. Regular users browse the catalog as before — only admins can add, edit, or remove anything.
- **Full CRUD for catalogs and products.** Through the admin panel, our team can add new catalogs, edit them in place, delete them safely (along with their products), and do the same for individual products inside each catalog.
- **Image uploads via Cloudinary.** Catalogs, products, and user profile pictures can now have real, custom images uploaded straight from the browser. Old images are automatically cleaned up when content is deleted or replaced.
- **Statistics.** The About page now shows live statistics about the whole platform: total catalogs, total products, total users, average products per catalog, and which catalog has the most/least products. Logged-in users also see their own personal statistics.
- **Search and sort in the admin panel.** Our admins can quickly find any catalog by title and sort the list by name or by number of products, ascending or descending.
- **Custom Handlebars helpers.** We wrote a `productCount` helper for friendly product counts ("Empty catalog", "1 product", "5 products"), plus the standard `uppercase` and `formatDate` helpers used across the app.
- **Password rules.** New accounts must use a password with at least 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, and a digit.
- **Error feedback on the login page.** Wrong password, weak password, or duplicate email all show a clear red message instead of a silent failure.
- **Profile picture in the menu.** When you log in, your avatar appears next to your name at the top right.
- **Responsive design improvements.** The new admin panel, login page, and statistics blocks all work cleanly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

# 📚 Getting Started
Want to run FerBromHata on your own machine? Here is the quickest way:

1. Make sure you have **Node.js (v18 or newer)** installed.
2. Clone the repository:
```bash
git clone https://github.com/konfixparadise/FerBromHata.git
```
3. Install all dependencies:
```bash
npm install
```
4. Create a `.env` file in the project root with your own Cloudinary credentials (you can sign up for free at [cloudinary.com](https://cloudinary.com/)):
```bash
CLOUDINARY_CLOUD_NAME=your_cloud_name
CLOUDINARY_API_KEY=your_api_key
CLOUDINARY_API_SECRET=your_api_secret
```
5. Start the app in development mode:
```bash
npm start
```
6. Open your browser at [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) — that's it!

To run in production mode, use `npm start` instead. The live version of our app is always available at [ferbromhata.onrender.com](https://ferbromhata.onrender.com/).

# 📱 Usage
There are three ways to use FerBromHata, depending on who you are:

**As a guest (not logged in)**
- Browse the homepage to read about our company and see live platform statistics.
- Explore the catalog, filter by category, and search for the solutions you need.
- Click into any catalog to see its products in detail.
- Visit the About page to learn our full history and view detailed app statistics.

**As a registered user**
- Everything a guest can do, plus:
- Your name and profile picture appear at the top of every page.
- The About page now also shows your personal statistics.
- You can log out at any time from the menu.

**As an admin**
- Everything a regular user can do, plus:
- The "Admin Panel" link appears in your menu, leading to FerBromHata Studio.
- From the Studio you can add, edit, or delete any catalog or product, with image upload included.
- The Studio comes with a built-in search and sort to make managing dozens of catalogs easy.

The default admin account for testing is `admin@admin.com` with password `admin`. Please change this in `models/user-store.json` for any real deployment!

# 🏢 Company
**FerBromHata** is a specialised firm dedicated to the design, creation, installation, and maintenance of modern agricultural infrastructure. Our company name combines three ideas: *Fer* (Farm), *Brom* (from the Bromus genus of grasses, our visual symbol), and *Hata* (Habitat — the new farm environment we want to build).

Our mission is simple but ambitious:
- Bring contemporary structural solutions to farms.
- Power those solutions with green energy — primarily wind and solar.
- Automate the hard work without losing workplaces, by retraining staff for higher-value roles.
- Upgrade existing farmsteads instead of forcing full reconstruction.

We have been in business since 1947, when our company was founded as a small producer of trusses for tractors and combines. After the Tokyo Summit on Green Energy in 1995, we rebranded into FerBromHata and refocused on sustainable, automated farming. In 2015 we partnered with the UCD Robotics and Engineering Department to test new machine solutions in real fields, and we have not stopped innovating since.

Today, our company supports farms across Dublin, Limerick, Waterford, and twenty more counties — with a workforce of 1000+ specialists and 5000+ units of our own equipment. We are currently expanding into international markets, with a special focus on South America and Australia.
